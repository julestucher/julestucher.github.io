Julia Tucher

Honors Mathematics and Computer Science student at Williams College ('21), Research Fellow at Williams College Mathematics Dept.

About me

Julia, born and raised in Oakland, Calif., is wrapping up her college career as a senior at Williams College. She has enjoyed taking full advantage of the liberal arts college model, exploring classes outside her majors ranging from Women's, Gender, and Sexuality Studies to Theater and from Psych to Poly Sci. Within her majors, Julia has benefitted from having close relationships with her professors, some of whom supported her in conducting independent as well as collaborative research. In CS, she has expanded her theoretical knowledge while developing broad range of technical skills. In math, she discovered the beauty of algebra, the joys of applications, and the pitfalls of real analysis.

Outside of classes, Julia took to the stage in department and student-led theatre productions. She can also be found enjoying the company of her improv group–Treestyle–or having a game night with friends. Her favorite college experience, though, has been acting as a Junior Advisor (JA) to the Class of 2023 and advising the JAs to the Class of 2024 while in her position on JA Advisory Board (JAAB).
